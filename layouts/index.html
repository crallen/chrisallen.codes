About Me

I am a software developer who has spent a lot of time working with web applications in the ecommerce space. More recently, I've taken a dive into the world of DevOps, with a focus on Amazon Web Services and tools like Docker and Chef. I work with a variety of technology stacks and languages, and I'm always on the lookout for something new to learn.

I grew up in the small town of Orange, Texas, and lived in the general Southeast Texas area for most of my life. I really got into video games at an early age, and it wasn't long after that I knew I wanted to make games for a living. This led me down the path of learning to code and while I didn't end up making games, I still found a career that continues to be challenging and rewarding. I now reside in the Austin area.

Away from the desk, I enjoy spending time with my wife and our dogs. We love to travel and really enjoy Walt Disney World and cruising.
